Ingredients

  • 4 pieces matzo
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons kosher for Passover vegetable oil

Matzah Brei

Description

 Matzah Brei is a dish of Ashkenazi Jewish origin made from matzah fried with eggs.

Directions
  1. Break the matzo into 1-inch pieces.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and pour over the matzos.
  3. Toss the matzo, drain off any excess.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork.
  5. Mix the eggs, salt and pepper into the matzo.
  6. Over the high heat, heat the butter and oil in a large saute pan.
  7. Add the matzo and fry until crisp.
  8. Flip over to fry the other side, breaking into pieces as it cooks.
  9. Serve with maple syrup, or preserves.
